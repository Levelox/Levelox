Privacy Policy

At Levelox Studios, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect and how it is used and protected.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use our services:

  • Contact details (e.g., name, email address) when you fill out forms.
  • Usage data (e.g., IP address, browser type, and interactions with our website).
  • Payment information for donations or purchases (processed securely through third-party services).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our website and services.
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • To process transactions and ensure security.
  • To analyze website usage for improvements.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law or to comply with legal obligations.
  • To trusted third-party services for secure payment processing.

4. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the information we hold about you.
  • Request corrections or deletions of your data.
  • Opt-out of data collection by contacting us at leveloxstudios@gmail.com.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at leveloxstudios@gmail.com.
