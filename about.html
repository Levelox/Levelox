About Levelox Studios

Founded on July 28, 2024, Levelox Studios began as a dream company fueled by inspiration from notable industry leaders such as JOGO Games, SuperJoy Studios, and GoodGamers. What started with $0 in funding has grown into a passionate endeavor to create immersive and engaging experiences for players worldwide.

At Levelox Studios, our journey began with a focus on developing Fortnite UGC (User-Generated Content) maps and crafting innovative web-based games. These games are designed to be played on computers, offering free entertainment for anyone looking to pass the time. Our mission is to make gaming accessible, enjoyable, and meaningful for players across the globe.

While we started small, our ambitions are anything but. We envision expanding beyond Fortnite and web games, venturing into new platforms, and eventually creating our own games on Steam. This progression represents our dedication to growth, creativity, and pushing the boundaries of what a small team can achieve.

Levelox Studios is more than just a company; it’s a vision, a dream to build something extraordinary from the ground up. Our goal is to become a recognized name in the gaming industry, connecting with players through innovative designs and captivating gameplay. We are committed to fostering a community that values creativity, inclusivity, and fun.

As we continue this journey, we remain driven by our passion for games, our commitment to quality, and the belief that even the smallest ideas can lead to the biggest successes. Welcome to Levelox Studios—where dreams and innovation come together.
